# 📐 Minimal Axiom Set

This page records the current minimal scaffold used across EUT-Core. The statements are intentionally compact and may be refined as formalization progresses.

## Axiom A1 — Harmonic Primacy
A fundamental harmonic field **H** exists and is taken as the primary ontological object for the framework.

## Axiom A2 — Resonant Dynamics
The admissible evolution of **H** is constrained by coherence and resonance principles that select stable or metastable structures.

## Axiom A3 — Emergent Geometry Map
A geometry map **Φ(H) → (g, ∇)** exists, relating harmonic structure to effective geometric data (metric and connection).

## Axiom A4 — Effective Matter Modes
Matter-like sectors correspond to stable, localized, or topologically protected harmonic structures within **H**.

## Axiom A5 — Coherence-Information Link
Information-theoretic and thermodynamic observables are encoded by coherence/decoherence structure in the harmonic substrate.

## Axiom A6 — Recovery and Limits
In appropriate regimes, EUT must recover established effective descriptions and observational constraints.

## Status Note

These axioms are currently a working scaffold. Formal statements, proofs, and compatibility conditions should be versioned in `theory/` as they mature.
